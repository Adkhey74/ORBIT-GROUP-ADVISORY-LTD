About Orbit Group

Independent by Design.

Orbit Group is an independent security consultancy supporting family offices, private offices and executive leadership across the UK and Europe.

We provide discreet security advisory, executive mobility and operational coordination through a deliberately selective model. From secure ground transportation and executive drivers to residential security support and trusted local partners, we deliver integrated solutions designed around each client's environment and operational requirements.

Rather than operating as a traditional security company, we act as a trusted extension of the private office — coordinating people, logistics and security with consistency, discretion and direct senior oversight.

Our approach is built on operational discipline, long-term relationships and the belief that the best security is measured not by its visibility, but by the confidence it provides.

Orbit Group is not a conventional security agency. We operate as a trusted operational network — built on direct relationships, selective mandates and an absolute standard of discretion. Every operator integrated into the network is treated as a partner, not an interchangeable resource.

02 —

03 —

London, Paris, the French Riviera, Geneva, Monaco, Ibiza — and beyond for the right profiles. Assignments can be short-term, recurring or seasonal.

05 —
Discretion and respect

Profiles we look for
Close Protection Officers

Experienced CPOs with a proven track record in UHNW or diplomatic environments. Discretion, composure and operational discipline are non-negotiable.

  • SIA CP licence or CNAPS A3P equivalent
  • Minimum 3 years active EP experience
  • UHNW, diplomatic or corporate background
  • First aid qualification (SST / TECC preferred)
  • English required — French, Spanish or Arabic a plus
Security Drivers & Chauffeurs

Protective drivers with executive chauffeur experience and genuine close protection awareness. The vehicle is an extension of the security operation.

  • Advanced or defensive driving qualification
  • Experience with UHNW or executive clients
  • Clean licence — PCO licence preferred (London)
  • Immaculate presentation and discretion
  • Counter-surveillance awareness
Maritime Security Specialists

Experienced operators with superyacht or maritime security backgrounds, comfortable in discreet high-expectation private client environments.

  • Close protection experience in maritime environments
  • STCW certification preferred
  • Mediterranean deployment experience
  • Seamless integration with yacht crew
  • Flexible seasonal availability
Local Partners & Fixers

Trusted ground-level contacts in our key operational zones who know their territory better than anyone.

  • Deep local knowledge in an Orbit coverage zone
  • Security, intelligence or law enforcement background
  • Discreet, reliable and professionally presented
  • Available for advance work and local coordination
  • References from previous private client mandates
